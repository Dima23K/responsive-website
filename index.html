About Us

The Woodstock tour is a walking tour around Woodstock to see the murals done by local and international artist like Freddy Sam, Andrzej Urbanski, Faith 47 and many more. The Khayelitsha Tour is a half day tour of murals done by local an international artists. The tour includes transport, a coffee, tshisa nyama and a beer. You also get to walk around and engage with the community of Khayelitsha and also have the opportunity to volunteer at a project or a school. Mzoli tour : we do offer a Mzoli VIP tour that you enjoy the township street party . For more information or to book a tour please feel free to contact us.
